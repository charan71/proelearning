BA Training Objectives

Session 1 (week 1)

  • Role of a Business Analyst (“BA”)
  • Structure of Development Team(Business/IT)
  • System Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
    • Requirement Gathering
    • Analysis and Design
    • Development/Implementation and Testing/QA
    • Deployment/Installation
    • Production (Business Use) / Maintenance / Support / Enhancements / Change Requests

  • JAD Session (“Joint Application Development”)

  • How to conduct JAD session?
  • Different Types of Requirements

    • Enterprise Architect

Session 5 (week 4)
