About The Course

This course focuses on the design, build, system administration and deployment of HPCM models and applications.Discover the tasks involved in preparing the structure and dimensionality of the application and the strategies for deployment using EPMA, SQL databases, Oracle Essbase and Life Cycle Management (LCM).

Learn about the drivers and assignments required to model data, how to execute the model and make amendments through SQL tables. This course also covers the analysis of data through Smart View and Financial Reports. You’ll get an opportunity to learn best practice with regard to validation of your models and explore security provisioning. This course provides everything you’ll need to manage or maintain models with Oracle’s HPCM Standard Profitability.

Learn the concepts required to design and develop HPCM models. Explore Essbase storage options, the management and maintenance of dimensionality, staged allocations and the deployment of HPCM applications.

  • Perform faster, easier multidimensional analysis and scenario modeling leveraging Oracle Essbase, the industry’s leading online analytical processing (OLAP) server
  • Enables users to create, maintain, and deploy cost and profitability models
  • Support multiple cost and revenue modeling approaches and create custom allocation methodologies
  • Drive rapid application design and iterations with a flexible business rules engine and intuitive interface, making it easy to build dimensions, hierarchies, metrics, and scenarios
  • Reveal a new level of transparency into cost and revenue allocations via traceability maps that can verify that business rules have been correctly applied
