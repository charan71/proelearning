About The Course

The CompTIA Security Plus SY0-501 certification course helps you prove your competency in topics such as threats, vulnerabilities, and attacks, system security, network infrastructure, access control, cryptography, risk management, and organizational security.

What is the Security+ Certification?

Security+ is a certification sponsored by CompTIA, the Computing Technology Industry Association. The Security+ Certification shows that you have the competence required to apply knowledge of security concepts, tools, and procedures to react to security incidents, as well as anticipating security risks and guarding against them before they occur. The goal is to test what is generally assumed to be the knowledge held by a security professional with two years of full-time security-related work experience. CompTIA began offering Security+ Certification back in 2002. The course is considered to be one of the leading vendor-neutral certification for security professionals.

Why get Security+ Certified?

Since the Security+ Certification program is an industry wide, vendor-neutral program developed by CompTIA (The Computing Technology Industry Association), getting certified will give you credentials that are highly regarded by the top technology companies such as Lockheed Martin, Hitachi Information Systems, Trendmicro, the U.S. State Department, and U.S. government contractors such as General Dynamics, EDS and Northrop Grumman. CompTIA Security+ is one of the options for certifications required by the U.S. Department of Defense (DoD), for military personnel or military contractors engaged in information assurance activities. According to CompTIA, over 45,000 individuals have obtained the CompTIA Security+ Certification.

CompTIA is a nonprofit, industry trade association. It consists of over 7,500 computer resellers, value-added resellers (VARs), distributors, manufacturers, and training companies all over the United States and Canada. Some of the members of CompTIA include Apple, Compaq, ATT, IBM, Intel, Microsoft, etc.

How can I get Security+ Certified?

If you are interested in becoming a Security+ certified professional, the information provided below will help you select products that are designed to assist you to learn the fundamental concepts of information security and prepare for the Security+ certification exam. You will learn about common common security threats, vulnerabilities, and weaknesses that exist in current systems, as well as security tools and methods, including sniffers, IDSs, active responses, and passive responses that can be used to secure them. The Security+ certification training course below will also provide all you need to know about access control and identity management, educating and protecting users, cryptography basics, physical and hardware-based security, and much more - all in a visual and easy to follow format. Once you have learned these skills, you will be ready to take the Security+ certification exam SY0-301.
