About The Course

Six Sigma is a set of tools and strategies for process improvement. And if we talk about the history , it was originally developed by Motorola in 1985.

It seeks to improve the quality of process output by identifying and removing errors just like we use the antivirus for our system to improve performance. The basic principle or we can say methodology followed by six sigma is DMAIC.

In simple terms we can say, Six Sigma is used to remove errors and defects from any process. It is a structured approach to problem solving that can be applied to any process - manufacturing, sales, marketing, IT, BPO, Accounting, Purchasing etc.

All processes have variation. And we all know variation is the cause of all evil - it leads to defects and customer dissatisfaction. Six Sigma methodology can be used to reduce variation from any source and thus improve costs, quality, and customer satisfaction.

And when we talk about Six sigma here are few things that should come to your mind:

  • Business process improvement methodology
  • 3.4 defects per million opportunities
  • Customer focused
  • Uses facts and data
  • Quantity financial benefits
  • Structured improvement approach
  • Value of Six Sigma is 99.99966%
