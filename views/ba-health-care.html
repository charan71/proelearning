About The Course

A Bachelors in Healthcare Administration degree is intended for individuals wanting to pursue careers dealing with the business operations of medical facilities. ... Students receive instruction on the fundamentals of business and the theories and practices applied to healthcare organizations.

Thanks to our know-how in automated mobile bases, BA HEALTHCARE designs, products and develops with your teams, mobile non-invasive medical and paramedic devices that meet the needs of patients and therapists.
