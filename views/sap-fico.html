About The Course

SAP FICO Stands for FI (Financial Accounting) and CO (Controlling). SAP FICO is the important module of ERP and both FI and CO modules stores the financial transactions data.

SAP FICO is the Finance and Cost controlling module in SAP ERP where FI stands for Financial Accounting and CO stands for Controlling. SAP FICO module is very robust and covers almost all business process encountered in various industries. It is one of the very important and widely implemented modules in SAP

Job Description of an SAP FICO consultant. ... Consultants play a more important role than end/power users in the company because they are responsible for the implementation and system configuration based on business requirements, GAAP analysis, improving processes, and looking for new opportunities/products.

FICO. FICO, also called Finance and Controlling, helps integrate the accounting side of a business. All the financial sections like Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, General Ledger, Tax, etc are incorporated into SAP. ... Thus, CO is a management tool which helps them analyze on how well the company is doing

It's been used in nearly 44 % of the world's ERP software using companies. SAP Software offers business a platform in the Form of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software to manage their separate database so that run the company. SAP is the acronym for Systems, Applications and Products.
