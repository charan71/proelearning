About The Course

A PeopleSoft database comprises of Three components, the PeopleSoft system catalog (or PeopleTools) , the application data and Meta data. The PeopleTools, similar in purpose to the Oracle system catalog, is a repository for the application (record, fields, panels, PeopleCode, etc.).

No doubt about being a good ERP product but Peoplesoft is on a slide that is tilted to only to go down. Oracle Applications EBS R12 is from Oracle own stable. ...Oracle is the company that owns PeopleSoft product. PeopleSoft is an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) software.

Oracle PeopleSoft Human Capital Management (HCM) is an on-premise suite of applications suitable for midsize to large organizations. The solution includes a Global Core Human Capital Management module as well as workforce management, labor rules and monitoring, workforce service delivery and talent management modules.

The PeopleSoft name and product line are now marketed by Oracle. PeopleSoftFinancial Management Solutions (FMS) and Supply Chain Management (SCM) are part of the same package, commonly known as Financials and Supply Chain Management (FSCM).
