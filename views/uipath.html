About The Course

Pro-elearning's Robotic Process Automation training will make you an expert in UiPath tool, you will become an expert in driving RPA initiatives in your organisation.

You'll master the concepts of key considerations while designing a RPA solution using UiPath, perform Image and text automation, create RPA Bots, perform Data Manipulation and debug and handle the exceptions, using real-life case studies.
