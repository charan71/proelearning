What is RPA?

Robotic Process Automation is a platform to reduce human efforts by deploying a Robot or Software to perfrom specific task which is repeatedly performed. RPA tools will interact with business applications as a human user would do.

Why should you learn Artificial Intelligence?

Robotic Process Automation field is considered to be very effective for career development. Keeping in mind of the future scope in this filed, one can expect numerous opportunities. Skilled resources would be offered very effective packages.

Who should learn Artificial Intelligence?

The one who wants to learn and excel their career in RPA should have to be Analytical and Logical. You have to be a good problem solver and has an ability to understand complex business processes and develop the programs.
