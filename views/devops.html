About The Course

DevOps training course is designed to help you become a DevOps practitioner and apply the latest in DevOps methodology to automate your software development lifecycle right out of the class.

You will master configuration management; continuous integration deployment, delivery and monitoring using DevOps tools such as Git, Docker, Jenkins, Puppet and Nagios in a practical, hands on and interactive approach.

The Devops training course focuses heavily on the use of Docker containers, a technology that is revolutionizing the way apps are deployed in the cloud today and is a critical skillset to master in the cloud age.

The modern organizations that adopt DevOps can achieve optimum performance by delivering with maximum speed, functionality, and innovation. It helps in maximizing employee satisfaction and engagement, and to propel business growth. No surprise that DevOps is being embraced by an increasing number of medium and large organizations today.
