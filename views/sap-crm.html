About The Course

It is a part of SAP Business Suite to manage customer relationship. It supports all customer-focused business areas such as marketing, sales and service. It is implemented for different customer interaction channels, such as Interaction Centre, Internet, and Mobile clients (hand-held devices like laptop, mobile, etc.).

SAP Cloud for Customer (SAP C4C) is a software as a service (SaaS) platform for sales and service. The platform is composed of SAP Cloud for Sales and SAP Cloudfor Service, which are marketed as separate entities by SAP. SAP Cloud for Customer runs on the SAP HANA Cloud Platform (HCP).

SAP CRM is the CRM tool provided by SAP and is used for many a business process. SAP CRM is a part of SAP business suite. It can implement customized business processes, integrate to other SAP and non-SAP systems, help achieve CRM strategies. SAP CRM can help an organization to stay connected to customers.

CRM is the Customer Relationship Management tool of SAP is the leading customer management tool across the globe. CRM activities of an organisation related to sales, marketing, service delivery and other client facing front end activities are automated and managed by SAP CRM tool.
