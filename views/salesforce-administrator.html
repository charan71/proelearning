About The Course

Salesforce Administration Essentials for Experienced Admins training lays emphasis on how to use Salesforce to meet the business needs. Participants will develop knowledge on the business process execution issues; their automation and troubleshooting.

This Salesforce advance admin training also explains the concepts and provides hands-on exposure on how to use analytics, improve data quality, and streamline requests with approval process.
